WELCOME TO OUR ORGANISATION

Become a Member

The Indian Peptide Society is a non-profit association. It was established to promote the advancement of education and research, in particular the scientific study of the Chemistry, Biochemistry and Biology of Peptides in India. The aim of the society is to provide a platform & common forum to the scientists working on Peptides in India, as well as to facilitate communication, cooperation & collaboration to national & international forums with respect to all aspects of the study on peptides.

Our Mission

The purpose of the society is to provide national and international forums to facilitate communication, cooperation and collaboration with respect to all aspects of the study of peptides. Members have an opportunity to actively participate in the emerging fields of peptide science.

Our Vision

Horizons of Indian Peptide Society are not restricted to the geographical boundaries of India but extend worldwide to Indian Peptide scientists who could also become members of the Indian Peptide Society and provide their valuable contributions.

Our Philosophy

The need of Indian Peptide Society was being felt for many years having looked at the growing number of Indian Peptide scientists in various peptide symposia worldwide and their growing contribution for research within India also.

FAQ

Indian Peptide Society was founded in 2006 under Societies Registration Act XXI of 1860 under Registrar of Societies Government of NCT of Delhi Registration No. S-59874. Objective of the society is to provide a platform & common forum to the scientists working on peptides in India and abroad to network, communicate and share knowledge and resources.

Scientists, researchers, faculty members, industry professionals, and students working in or interested in peptide science can become members of the Indian Peptide Society. Membership is open to individuals in India and abroad who share the society's objectives.

Yes, students pursuing research or studies in peptide science, chemistry, biochemistry, or related fields are welcome to join IPS. Student membership provides access to symposia, networking opportunities, and the broader peptide science community.
